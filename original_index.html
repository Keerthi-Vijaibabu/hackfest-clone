About The Hackathon

PSG iTech Hackfest 2024 sponsored by SAP is the third edition of a national-level Hackathon, which provides a platform to bring talented students from diverse backgrounds together to collaborate and create innovative solutions to real-world problems. Participants will have the opportunity to interact with industry experts, mentors, and judges who will provide them with valuable insights and feedback on their projects and ideas.

  • Date: 4th March to 26th July 2024
  • Team Size: 2 to 5 members
  • Free registrations
  • Prize pool of 6000 USD
